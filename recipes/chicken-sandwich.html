Chicken Sandwich

Description

The chick fil a classic, Chicken Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Chicken cutlet
  • Lettuce
  • Mayonnaise
  • Pickles
  • Eggs
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Season the chicken cutlets to your heart desires
  2. Put the Seasoned chicken in the eggs and the flour (do it twice)
  3. Fry the chicken until cooked fully through at 165 degrees
  4. Now build the sandwich
