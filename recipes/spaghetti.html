Spaghetti

Description

This nice traditional dish is an italian favorite of mine, Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Beef
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti noodles in salted water
  2. Cook the ground beef until fully cooked through
  3. Once both of those are mix them together with the marinara sauce
  4. Sprinkle the parmesan on top
